M/S Ritika Industries

The Company has a wide range of metal hardware in brass, bronze, malleable iron and aluminum. The products are made by casting, stamping, hand forging, fabrication and die casting. Some of the designs are standard while others are as per client specifications. These are for varied applications such as for doors, windows, shutters, cabinets, bath, decorative shelf, trunk and gate applications etc. There is a total range of about 6000 items. Most designs can be done in any metal / finish. The Company is always keen to take up new development / production work and have invested heavily in special tooling and dies.
